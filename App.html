The Challenge & The Solution

The primary challenge was a time-consuming, error-prone manual reconciliation process for monthly claims reports. The solution was to develop an automated, SQL-driven system that streamlined this workflow, ensuring data integrity and speed. The diagram below illustrates the transformation of the process.

After: Automated System
